I like *thematic* Wordle plays, where the first 2 or 3 guesses are thematically related.  It certainly helps for the 2 words to be mostly-distinct, still, but I feel you can enjoy the math of the pattern matching along with a small bit of word play.  Anyways:

  1.  LUNAR DEITY (my favorite)

  2.  LEMON SUGAR

  3.  MOUSE RANCH

  4.  MOUSE PINKY & MOUSE BRAIN

  5.  AUDIO RHYME (my second favorite)

  6.  MAINE TOURS (sadly Wordle does not accept MAINE, yet)

  7.  BLIMP HASTE & HASTY BLIMP

  8.  BOGUS EMAIL

  9.  FRESH AUDIO

 10.  VIDEO TRASH

I think it's fully within "the rules" for Wordle to change the wordlist, adds/deletes/anything.  So I'm a bit open-minded about the boundary of the Wordle word set (for some not-quite-right definition of "open set" :P).  Maybe they never will change it, though.

I don't expect AOEUI DHTNS to hit the word list any time soon, of course, but MAINE seems fine, right?
